Task 4: Personalized Outreach Angles (Top 30 Only)
For each Tier A clinic, create:

4a. Personalized Angle (1 per clinic)
Write one specific observation that can be used in the opening line of an outreach message. This must reference something real and verifiable about that specific clinic.

Good examples:

"Your Google reviews show patients love your cosmetic work, but several mention difficulty reaching you by phone"
"Your Instagram shows beautiful implant cases, but I couldn't find a WhatsApp number on your site"
"I tried calling your number from your Google listing and it rang out after 30 seconds"
"Your 4.8 rating is excellent, but the 3 most recent 1-star reviews all mention phone issues"
Bad examples (do NOT write these):

"Your clinic is great" (generic)
"You might be missing calls" (assumption without evidence)
"AI can help your clinic" (they don't care about AI)
4b. Three Message Drafts Per Clinic
For each Tier A clinic, draft:
